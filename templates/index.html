Keras2C Converter

Convert your Keras `.h5` models to C code instantly!

How to Use:

  1. Click the "Choose File" button to upload your Keras `.h5` model.
  2. Ensure the model file is valid and does not exceed 100 MB.
  3. Enter the desired function name for the conversion.
  4. Check the box to confirm you understand that your data is not stored.
  5. Click "Convert to C" to start the conversion process.
  6. Download the generated `.c` file when the conversion is complete.
